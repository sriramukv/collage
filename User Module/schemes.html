SCHEMES
The Indian government, at all levels, periodically releases Welfare Schemes for various segments of the population. These programmes may be coordinated by the federal government, state-specific, or a combination of the two. We have made an effort to give you a simple, one-stop location to learn about the government's welfare programmes and all of their features, such as facts on the programmes, eligible beneficiaries, and benefit categories.

Natioanl Pension Schemes

With the release of Ministry of Finance (Department of Economic Affairs) Notification No. 5/7/2003-ECB & PR on December 22, 2003, the National Pension System (NPS) was made available to Central Government employees. The PFRDA Act of 2013 and the regulations created by PFRDA and the Department of Financial Services currently govern NPS.
